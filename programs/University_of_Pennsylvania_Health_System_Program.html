Nicole Aqui, MD is the Program Director at University of Pennsylvania Health System Program, located in Philadelphia, PA.

Director Information

Dr. Nicole Alyse Aqui, MD
- Position:
- Associate Professor of Clinical Pathology and Laboratory Medicine
- Vice Chair for Inclusion, Diversity, and Equity
- Director, Pathology Residency Training Program
- Chief of Transfusion and Apheresis Services
- Affiliation: Perelman School of Medicine at the University of Pennsylvania
- Specialty Division: Transfusion Medicine & Therapeutic Pathology
- Clinical Expertise: Transfusion medicine, apheresis, blood banking, hematopoietic stem cell and immune effector cell collection/processing, sickle cell disease
- Education:
- B.S. (Cellular and Molecular Biology): University of Maryland College Park, 1996
- M.D.: University of Pennsylvania School of Medicine, 2001
- Postgraduate Training:
- Student Fellowship: Department of Pathology and Laboratory Medicine, Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania, 1999-2000
- Resident, Clinical Pathology: Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania, 2001-2004
- Fellow, Patient Oriented Training Program: University of Pennsylvania, 2003-2004
- Fellow, Division of Transfusion Medicine: Department of Pathology and Laboratory Medicine, Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania, 2004-2005
- Postdoctoral Research Fellow: University of Pennsylvania, 2005-2006
- Awards and Honors: Dr. Aqui has received numerous awards, including the Medical Society of Eastern Pennsylvania Medical Student Award for Leadership and Community Service, Helen O. Dickens Award, Charles Drew Award, and the David Goodman, M.D., Ph.D. Award for University Service.
- Memberships and Professional Organizations: Dr. Aqui is a member of several professional organizations, including the College of American Pathologists, International Society of Cellular Therapy, AABB, American Society for Hematology, and the Society of Black Pathologists.
- Location: Perelman School of Medicine, University of Pennsylvania
- Contact: Email

Dr. Nicole Alyse Aqui is an Associate Professor of Clinical Pathology and Laboratory Medicine at the Perelman School of Medicine at the University of Pennsylvania. She specializes in transfusion medicine, apheresis, and blood banking. Dr. Aqui has made significant contributions to her field and has been recognized with numerous awards and honors for her work.
